We wanted to say "Thank You!"

Team 1241 - Theory 6

Theory 6 graciously donated a spare chassis to us. Their example of gracious professionalism throughout the competition is much appreciated, and we look forward to seeing them in Worlds.
Thank you Team 1241!

Team 4627 - Manning Robotics

Manning Robotics led our alliance to the finals. Without their help, we would not have been Canadian Pacific finalists, or be going to Houston. We look forward to seeing them in Worlds.
Thank You Team 4627!

Team 1868 - Space Cookies

This team inspired us all, not only by being an all girls team, but being amazing members of their community. They not only extended a hand of good-sportsmanship during regionals, but went above and beyond, even after the competition. We strive to be like them and have an impact on our community.
Thank You Team 1868!

Team 2643 - Dark Matter

Dark Matter set an example of safety and hospitality during regionals. We wish to follow in their footsteps in ensuring that future FIRST Robotics Competitions are just as friendly and welcoming.
Thank You Team 2643!
