Our Robot - "Flippy Boi"

The Flipper


The flipper is comprised of a stiff plywood board actuated by a high-powered pneumatic piston. When the 60 psi of pressure is let through the solenoid valve that sits inside the robot, the flipper throws a powercube into the switch. This mechanism has never failed in a robotics competition.

The Chassis


The flipper sits atop a robust aluminum chassis. The six belt-driven wheels are controlled by four 337 Watt CIM motors which gives our robot more than enough power, speed, and agility to quickly drive from portal to switch and back again.

The Design


We decided to make our robot modular to make it easier to fix and improve. Our entire flipper can be removed easily with four nuts, giving us access to all of the electronics in the chassis.
